Reconciliation Note

Observation: A variance of ₹ 12,45,000.00 is observed in the 'Service Revenue - Consulting' ledger when compared against the consolidated GSTR-3B outward supplies for the period April 2023 to March 2024.

Causal Analysis: This discrepancy arises due to unbilled revenue recognized in the books of accounts as per Ind AS 115 requirements at the financial year-end. The corresponding tax invoices for these consulting milestones were physically raised in April 2024 and subsequently reported in the GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B of the subsequent financial year (FY 2024-25).

Audit Action Required: Ensure this amount is appropriately disclosed under Schedule III of GSTR-9 (Annual Return) as "Unbilled revenue at the end of Financial Year" to prevent departmental notices regarding turnover mismatch during assessment.
