Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data hosted in India? expand_more
Yes, all ComplianceOS data is hosted on local Indian servers to ensure compliance with data localization laws and to provide the lowest latency possible for our users.
Can I import data from Tally or Zoho? expand_more
Absolutely. We offer one-click migration tools for Tally, Zoho Books, and Quickbooks, ensuring you can transition your entire financial history without losing a single entry.
Is the Pro plan MCA compliant? expand_more
Yes, our Pro and Business plans include the mandatory Audit Trail (edit log) feature as per the latest Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) guidelines.
Do you offer support for GST filing? expand_more
We provide automated generation of GSTR-1, 2, and 3B reports. For the Pro plan and above, we also offer direct filing via our secure API gateway.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? expand_more
You retain read-only access to your data for up to 7 years, as required by Indian tax laws. You can also export all your data in Excel or PDF formats at any time.
Can my CA access my account for free? expand_more
Yes, every paid plan includes one complimentary "Auditor Seat" specifically designed for your Chartered Accountant to review books and pull reports.
