Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2024. This document details how ComplianceOS utilizes tracking technologies to maintain ledger integrity and improve your experience.

1. Introduction

ComplianceOS ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform. This policy explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

Given the nature of our financial compliance services, certain cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of the platform, ensuring secure session management and data integrity.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

security

Essential Cookies

Crucial for core functions like security, network management, and accessibility. You may disable these by changing your browser settings, but this may affect how the platform functions.

  • Session_ID (Authentication)
  • CSRF_Token (Security)
  • Load_Balancer_Route
analytics

Performance & Analytics

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They assist in improving the user experience.

  • _ga (Google Analytics)
  • _gid (Session Tracking)
  • Usage_Metrics

3. Managing Your Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie rights by setting your preferences in the Cookie Consent Manager, which allows you to select which categories of cookies you accept or reject.

Essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are strictly necessary to provide you with services. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.
