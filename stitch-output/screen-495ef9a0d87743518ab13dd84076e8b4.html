Our Genesis

We built the accounting software we wish existed when we started our businesses.

The compliance gap in India’s digital era.

For years, Indian entrepreneurs have been forced to choose between legacy software that feels like it was designed in the 1990s or global platforms that don't understand the nuances of GST, TDS, and the complexities of the Indian fiscal year.

We saw founders spending more time in spreadsheets than in their products. We saw CAs frustrated by fragmented data and manual reconciliations. The system wasn't just broken; it was indifferent to the speed at which modern Indian business moves.

ComplianceOS was born in a small office in Bengaluru out of this frustration. We didn't want to build just another tool. We wanted to build a financial operating system that respects the precision of Indian accounting while delivering the utility of modern software.
